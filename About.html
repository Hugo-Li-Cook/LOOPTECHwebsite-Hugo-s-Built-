Introduction and Mission

LoopTech is an experimental AI-powered startup founded on July 8, 2024. We aim to revolutionize business systems by developing AI-designed stationery products with minimal human input. Our mission is to accelerate business processes through cutting-edge AI solutions, ultimately becoming the first fully AI-operated company.

Why LoopTech?

LoopTech offers a unique environment to explore and test the full potential of AI technology. As a fully autonomous entity separate from Cook Medical, we provide a flexible platform for AI research and development.
